What is Doodle on the Table?



A doodle, for a fan, on a birthday

Doodle on the Table is a way to show a true fan something special on their birthday

For the true fans birthday and how it works:

selecting the winners:

every Saturday morning I roll a seven sided dice 3 times on Instagram stories. Numbers that land reflect days of the week. 3 winners every week. 3 doodles every week.

Numbers on dy reflect days as seen below:

  1. Sunday

  2. Monday

  3. Tuesday

  4. Wednesday

  5. Thursday

  6. Friday

  7. Saturday

The number(s) / day(s) that win reflect the following weeks winning days / birthday gift days.

Keep in mind with shipping you may receive your birthday doodle gift after, before, or on the day of your birthday!

How to become a winner:

For every fan that buys a physical hard copy cd of (2) albums from this website (don’t buy it off of iTunes, Spotify, etc….) and (2) different physical apparel items (shirts, sweatshirts, hoodies, tank tops, jackets…. (basically what is worn on your chest) you can become a winner.

*(must be 2 different chest wear items in the same size! from our merch/apparel shop on this website.)

*Important: When you make your purchase(s) save (write down) your promo code(s) at the bottom of receipts. Must have 4 promo codes at the minimum. If making multiple purchases of items at the same time, the amount of items will reflect the amount of promo codes on receipts.

Every year you don’t win on your birthday:

if it is your birthday and somebody else has the same birthday who wins….your chances increase in the future if the dy roll falls on your birthday. The winner is selected at random and anyone can win, but the more years you don’t win you get more ping pong balls in the lottery

Doodle on the Table envelope

Beware: Do not open until your birthday! If it is your birthday or was your birthday recently, happy birthday or belated birthday and open immediately!!!

What you need to provide when you make your account (4 things):

  1. Your first name, date of birth, and the year you were born

    (We only collect the year to get your age correct, we’re a band not going to profit off selling information, this is for the true fan!)

  2. Your mailing address or P.O. Box (This is so we can send you the physical copy of the doodle as well as certificate of authenticity.)

  3. Your Instagram name and email address: (we collect this to 1) let you know if you have become the winner and 2) to add you to exclusive close friends stories for only winners on Instagram. Close friends on stories can see behind the scenes footage only seen on close friends, limited edition merch (1 of a 100) when offered, 24 hour advanced notice of recently added shows in or around your area (100 miles from address or P.O. Box given or if it is in your current U.S.A. state). *If you move to another state or need to change your address, you can update that info in your login page.

  4. Your favorite animal (this is for the doodle!)

If you become a winner you can not win twice and are no longer eligible. All winners will be provided with the artwork and a certification of authenticity as well as a shoutout if you do display winning publicly on Instagram. Thank you and good luck
